/// WHO WE ARE ///

MECH ZONE AUTOMOTIVE PVT. LTD.

Welcome to MECH ZONE AUTOMOTIVE PVT. LTD., a trusted and growing automobile service brand based in Kanchipuram, Tamil Nadu, India (631551).

With a strong foundation built over 30+ years of experience, we are committed to delivering high-quality automotive solutions for both personal and commercial vehicles.

OUR LEGACY

Our journey began as Sri Saravana Auto Works, a well-known service center that earned customer trust through dedication, skill, and consistent service quality. Today, we are evolving into MECH ZONE AUTOMOTIVE PVT. LTD., a modern automotive brand with a vision to expand and innovate across the industry.

30+ YEARS OF EXCELLENCE

OUR VISION

To become one of the leading automotive service brands in Tamil Nadu, known for:

  • Advanced service technology
  • Reliable and transparent work
  • Customer-first approach
  • High-quality standards

OUR MISSION

To deliver reliable, affordable, and high-quality automotive services while building long-term relationships with our customers. Our team ensures every vehicle is handled with precision, care, and expertise.

EXPANSION PLAN

We aim to expand MECH ZONE AUTOMOTIVE PVT. LTD. across Tamil Nadu through a robust franchise network, making professional and trusted automobile services accessible everywhere.
